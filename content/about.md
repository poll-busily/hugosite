## AI Content
A few short years ago I wouldn't have needed to say anything on the matter but strange times are ahead for creators. So here it is: none of the content on the site has been created using AI, either wholly or with the use of AI assistance. My view is that it can be a fine tool for work but that - at the very least - our voices, imperfect as they may be, must remain our own.
